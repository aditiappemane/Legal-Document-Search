REGISTRATION ACT (1908) Section 17  Documents of which registration is compulsory  (1)
The following documents shall be registered if the property to which they relate is
situate in a district in which, and if they have been executed on or after the date on
which this Act came into force, namely: (a) instruments of gift of immovable property; (b)
other non testamentary instruments that create, declare, assign, limit or extinguish
rights in immovable property valued at 100 or more; (c) non testamentary instruments
acknowledging receipt/payment of consideration relating to such rights; (d) leases of
immovable property from year to year, or for any term exceeding one year, or reserving a
yearly rent; and (e) instruments transferring or assigning any decree or court order
affecting immovable property valued at 100 or more.
