COURT FEES ACT (1870) Section 7  Computation of fees payable in certain suits  The amount
of fee payable under this Act in the suits hereinafter mentioned shall be computed as
follows: (i) In suits for money (including damages, compensation, arrears of maintenance,
annuities or other sums payable periodically)  according to the amount claimed; (ii) In
suits for maintenance or annuities  ten times the amount claimed to be payable for one
year; (iii) In suits regarding movable property having market value  according to such
value at the date of presenting the plaint; (iv) In suits regarding movable property of no
market value (e.g., documents relating to title)  fee as prescribed under this section.
